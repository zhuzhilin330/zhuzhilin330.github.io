Review 合同是经济活动中极为关键的法律文件，关乎交易双方的权益与责任。 Contracts are extremely crucial legal documents in economic activities, concerning the rights, interests and responsibilities of both trading parties. 律师在合同起草环节，会依据客户具体需求与交易背景，精准把控合同架构与条款。 When drafting contracts, lawyers will accurately control the contract structure and terms according to the specific needs of clients and the transaction background. 首先，明确合同主体信息，确保主体资格合法合规，避免因主体瑕疵导致合同无效。 Firstly, clarify the information of the contract parties to ensure the legality and compliance of the subject qualifications and avoid the invalidity of the contract due to subject defects. 接着，详细拟定交易标的条款，清晰界定货物规格、服务内容等，防止模糊不清引发纠纷。 Then, carefully draw up the terms of the subject matter of the transaction, clearly define the specifications of goods, service content, etc., to prevent disputes caused by ambiguity. 在价款支付条款上，规定支付方式、时间节点与违约责任，保障客户资金安全与交易顺利推进。 In the payment terms, stipulate the payment method, time nodes and liability for breach of contract to ensure the safety of clients' funds and the smooth progress of the transaction. 例如，在大型设备采购合同起草中，律师不仅要明确设备型号、质量标准，还需对交付时间、安装调试责任以及质保期等关键环节细致规定。 For example, when drafting a large - scale equipment procurement contract, lawyers not only need to clarify the equipment model and quality standards, but also need to carefully stipulate key links such as the delivery time, installation and commissioning responsibilities, and the quality guarantee period. 合同审查时，律师会从法律合规性、风险防控与商业合理性多维度考量。 When reviewing contracts, lawyers will consider from multiple dimensions such as legal compliance, risk prevention and control, and business rationality. 检查合同是否符合现行法律法规，杜绝违法条款。 Check whether the contract complies with current laws and regulations and eliminate illegal clauses. 针对潜在风险点，如不可抗力条款是否完备、争议解决方式是否有利等进行排查与修正。 Investigate and correct potential risk points, such as whether the force majeure clause is complete and whether the dispute resolution method is advantageous. 同时，从商业角度审视合同条款是否契合客户经营策略与利益诉求。 At the same time, examine from a business perspective whether the contract terms are in line with the client's business strategy and interest demands. 以常见的租赁合同审查为例，律师会关注租金递增方式、房屋维修责任划分、转租限制等条款，为客户规避风险并争取最大利益。 Taking the review of a common lease contract as an example, lawyers will pay attention to clauses such as the rent increase method, the division of house maintenance responsibilities, and sub - lease restrictions to avoid risks for clients and strive for maximum benefits. 项目法律事务处理 Handling of Project Legal Affairs 在企业并购项目里，律师首先开展法律尽职调查。 In enterprise merger and acquisition projects, lawyers first carry out legal due diligence. 全面梳理目标企业的股权结构，核查股权是否存在质押、代持纠纷等问题，确保收购方取得清晰股权。 Comprehensively sort out the equity
